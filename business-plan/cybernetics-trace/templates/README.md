## Usage Instructions

### For Sales Templates

1. **Discovery Call Notes** — Fill out during every discovery call
2. **Proposal Template** — Customize after qualified call, send within 24 hours
3. **Statement of Work** — Send with proposal or after verbal acceptance
4. **Client Questionnaire** — Send after contract signed

### For Delivery Templates

1. **Audit Framework** — Your master checklist for the entire engagement
2. **Systems Inventory** — Fill out during Days 4-10
3. **Security Assessment** — Fill out during Days 7-8
4. **Vendor Contact List** — Fill out during Days 2-10
5. **Executive Summary** — Create during Days 11-14, present Days 15-18

## Template Customization

### Before First Use

1. Replace `[YOUR NAME]` with your name
2. Replace `[YOUR EMAIL]` with your email
3. Replace `[YOUR PHONE]` with your phone
4. Replace `[YOUR WEBSITE]` with your website
5. Add your logo/branding if desired

### For Each Client

1. Replace all `[BRACKETED TEXT]` with client-specific info
2. Delete instruction blocks before sending
3. Verify pricing matches your current rates
4. Customize sections based on client industry/needs

## Quality Checklist

Before sending any document to a client:

- [ ] All [BRACKETED TEXT] replaced
- [ ] Client name spelled correctly throughout
- [ ] Dates are accurate
- [ ] Pricing matches agreed terms
- [ ] Contact information is current
- [ ] No spelling/grammar errors
- [ ] Instruction blocks deleted
- [ ] Formatting is clean and professional

## File Naming Convention

When saving client-specific documents:

```

Examples:
AcmeManufacturing-Proposal-2026-01-15.md
AcmeManufacturing-ExecutiveSummary-2026-02-01.md
AcmeManufacturing-SOW-2026-01-15.md
```
