## Executive Summary

This roadmap provides the week-by-week execution plan for launching Cybernetics Trace Technology Partners. Each week contains specific, actionable tasks with clear success criteria.

**End Goal (Day 90):**
- Business fully operational
- 3 audits sold and/or delivered
- 1 client converted to retainer
- Sustainable pipeline for continued growth

## Contingency Plans

### If Pipeline Is Slow (Weeks 4-6)

**Indicators:**
- Less than 5% connection acceptance rate
- Zero discovery calls by Day 35
- Zero proposals by Day 42

**Actions:**
1. A/B test connection request messaging
2. Increase connection request volume to 75/week
3. Add phone outreach to mix
4. Accelerate networking events
5. Launch referral partner outreach
6. Consider LinkedIn Premium/Sales Navigator

### If Closes Are Slow (Weeks 7-10)

**Indicators:**
- Zero closes by Day 55
- Close rate below 20%
- Prospects stalling at proposal stage

**Actions:**
1. Review and refine discovery call approach
2. Analyze lost proposals for patterns
3. Consider pricing adjustment
4. Strengthen social proof (testimonials, case studies)
5. Add urgency mechanisms to proposals
6. Request feedback from lost prospects

### If Delivery Is Overwhelming (Weeks 9-12)

**Indicators:**
- Delivery timelines slipping
- Quality concerns
- Sales activities declining

**Actions:**
1. Pause new proposals temporarily
2. Block calendar for delivery focus
3. Create delivery shortcuts where possible
4. Consider subcontracting specific tasks
5. Extend delivery timelines for new clients
6. Batch similar work across clients
