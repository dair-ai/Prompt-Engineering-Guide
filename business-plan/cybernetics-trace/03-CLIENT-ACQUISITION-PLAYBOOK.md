## 3. Discovery Call Framework

### 3.1 Call Objectives

**Primary:** Understand their pain deeply enough to know if they're a fit
**Secondary:** Demonstrate expertise through expert questions, not answers
**Tertiary:** Set up the audit proposal as the logical next step

**Key Principle:** You are the doctor. Diagnose first. Prescribe later.

### 3.2 Pre-Call Preparation (10 minutes)

**Research Checklist:**
- [ ] Company website reviewed
- [ ] LinkedIn profile reviewed
- [ ] Recent news/press releases checked
- [ ] Company size confirmed
- [ ] Key systems guessed (ERP likely in use?)
- [ ] 2-3 industry-specific questions prepared

**Meeting Setup:**
- Video on (if they're comfortable)
- Quiet environment
- Notes template ready
- Calendar open for scheduling next step

### 3.3 Discovery Call Structure (15-20 minutes)

**Opening (2 minutes):**
```
"[First Name], thanks for taking the time. I know [CFOs/COOs]

about what's happening at [Company] so I can see if there's

```

```

    and what's your role in keeping things running?"
```

- [ ] Clear operational pain point
