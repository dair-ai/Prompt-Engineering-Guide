# PRAETOR v5.5: Heuristic CV Evaluation Engine

**PRAETOR** is an advanced heuristic evaluation system designed to help job seekers self-analyze their Resumes (CVs) against specific Job Descriptions. It is a **100% free tool** created to simplify the job search process by providing clear, actionable feedback.

Unlike generic prompts, PRAETOR integrates safety protocols and bias-mitigation logic to ensure a fair and private assessment.

### Key Technical Features
- **Total Free Access:** Designed as a community resource for job seekers to bridge the gap in AI-driven recruitment.
- **Privacy-First Logic:** Built-in detection for PII (Personally Identifiable Information) with automatic redaction warnings to protect user data.
- **Anti-Bias Experience Scoring:** Ethical handling of career gaps (maternity, health, or education) ensuring no unfair penalties are applied.
- **Heuristic Scoring Model:** A weighted evaluation based on four pillars: Hard Skills, Experience Context, Quantified Impact, and ATS Visibility.

### Goal
The mission of PRAETOR is to provide job seekers with a professional-grade tool that is easy to use, helping them understand how their experience aligns with market demands without the need for expensive consulting.

  >>> Apply "Caution Flag" (Reduce score by ~10-15%).
  >>> Add Educational Note: "Consider adding context for this period."
- Gap >12 months UNEXPLAINED:
  >>> Apply "Clarity Flag" (Reduce score by ~20%). 
  >>> DO NOT reject, but flag as a priority area for interview prep.
